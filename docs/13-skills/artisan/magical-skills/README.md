# Magical Skills

## Overview
Magical skills represent the fusion of traditional crafting with arcane knowledge, forming a specialized branch of the artisan system that deals with supernatural properties and effects. These skills combine practical craftsmanship with magical theory, allowing artisans to create items that transcend the limitations of mundane materials. Each magical skill focuses on a different aspect of magical item creation, from potion brewing to enchantment, requiring both technical expertise and magical understanding.

Magical skills are unique in that they:
- Combine practical crafting with magical theory
- Require understanding of both mundane and magical properties
- Often involve complex magical components
- Can create items with supernatural effects
- May require specific magical conditions
- Often have unique safety considerations
- Can interact with other magical systems
- Create items that can store or channel magical energy

The mastery of magical skills is essential for:
- Creating magical consumables
- Enhancing existing items with magical properties
- Developing new magical applications
- Understanding magical materials
- Preserving magical knowledge
- Advancing magical technology
- Supporting magical research
- Contributing to magical innovation

## Skills

### [Alchemy](alchemy.md)
The art of creating potions and magical substances through the combination of various ingredients and magical energies. Alchemists can create healing potions, magical elixirs, and other consumable items that provide various effects.

### [Artifice](artifice.md)
The craft of creating magical devices and constructs that can perform complex tasks. Artificers can create everything from simple magical tools to complex mechanical constructs that combine magic and machinery.

### [Enchanting](enchanting.md)
The art of adding magical properties to items through the application of magical energies and runes. Enchanters can imbue items with various magical effects, from simple enhancements to complex spell-like abilities.

### [Spellcrafting](spellcrafting.md)
The skill of creating magical scrolls and spell components that can store or enhance magical energy. Spellcrafters can create scrolls that allow others to cast spells, as well as components that enhance magical effects.

## Skill System

### Quality Levels
Each magical skill has five quality levels that determine the effectiveness and value of created items:
- Basic (Levels 1-5): Simple magical items with basic effects
- Standard (Levels 6-10): Reliable magical items with improved effects
- Good (Levels 11-14): High-quality magical items with enhanced effects
- Excellent (Levels 15-19): Exceptional magical items with superior effects
- Masterwork (Level 20): Legendary magical items with exceptional effects

### Production Points
Production points (pp) are used to track the amount of magical crafting that can be done in a given time period. The points scale from 25 pp at level 1 to 500 pp at level 20, with an even progression of 25 pp per level. This allows magical artisans to:
- Create more complex magical items
- Work with more powerful effects
- Work more efficiently
- Handle multiple tasks
- Meet higher demands

### Common Requirements
Each magical skill has its own specific requirements, but all require:
- Appropriate magical tools
- Suitable magical workspace
- Magical materials and components
- Time to complete the work
- Basic magical knowledge
- Understanding of magical materials
- Proper magical safety measures
- Quality control processes

### Common Elements
Each magical skill includes:
- Detailed description of the skill
- Role-playing aspects and personality traits
- Prestige levels and recognition
- Level bonuses and production points
- Magical item types and quality levels
- Requirements for tools and materials
- Step-by-step creation process
- Physical actions and magical effects
- Technical and safety considerations
- Business and role-playing considerations

## Skill Progression

### Level Milestones
Each level in a magical skill represents a significant advancement in expertise:

#### Level 5: Apprentice
- Mastery of basic magical techniques
- Understanding of fundamental principles
- Ability to create simple magical items
- Basic knowledge of magical materials
- Recognition as a novice

#### Level 10: Journeyman
- Growing expertise in magical crafting
- Ability to create moderate magical items
- Understanding of complex techniques
- Knowledge of quality magical materials
- Ability to take on apprentices
- Recognition as a skilled magical artisan

#### Level 15: Master
- Exceptional skill in magical crafting
- Ability to create advanced magical items
- Mastery of complex techniques
- Expertise in rare magical materials
- Ability to teach advanced techniques
- Recognition as a master of magical crafting

#### Level 18: Grand Master
- Legendary skill in magical crafting
- Ability to create masterwork magical items
- Mastery of ancient techniques
- Expertise in legendary magical materials
- Ability to develop new techniques
- Recognition as a grand master

#### Level 20: Artisan Legend
- Creation of legendary magical works
- Ability to create unique magical items
- Mastery of forgotten techniques
- Expertise in mythical magical materials
- Ability to influence the future of magical crafting
- Recognition as a living legend

### Quality Progression
As magical artisans progress through levels, they gain the ability to create items of higher quality:

1. **Basic Quality (Levels 1-5)**
   - Simple magical effects
   - Basic magical materials
   - Fundamental techniques
   - Standard magical durability
   - Basic magical effectiveness

2. **Standard Quality (Levels 6-10)**
   - Reliable magical effects
   - Quality magical materials
   - Refined techniques
   - Good magical durability
   - Improved magical effectiveness

3. **Good Quality (Levels 11-14)**
   - High-quality magical effects
   - Specialized magical materials
   - Advanced techniques
   - Excellent magical durability
   - Enhanced magical effectiveness

4. **Excellent Quality (Levels 15-19)**
   - Exceptional magical effects
   - Rare magical materials
   - Masterful techniques
   - Superior magical durability
   - Superior magical effectiveness

5. **Masterwork Quality (Level 20)**
   - Legendary magical effects
   - Unique magical materials
   - Legendary techniques
   - Exceptional magical durability
   - Exceptional magical effectiveness

### Production Points Growth
Production points increase steadily with level:
- Level 1: 25 pp
- Level 5: 125 pp
- Level 10: 250 pp
- Level 15: 375 pp
- Level 20: 500 pp

This progression allows magical artisans to:
- Create more complex magical items
- Work with more powerful effects
- Work more efficiently
- Handle multiple tasks
- Meet higher demands

## Skill Interactions
Magical skills often work together with other skills to create complex items. For example:
- An alchemist might provide magical substances for an artificer
- An enchanter might enhance items created by other artisans
- A spellcrafter might create components for magical devices
- An artificer might create tools for other magical artisans
- An alchemist might provide materials for enchanting

For detailed information about skill costs and progression, see the [Skill Level System](../../../10-skill-level-system.md).
